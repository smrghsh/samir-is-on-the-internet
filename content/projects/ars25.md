AI War Cloud is an [award-winning](https://starts-prize.aec.at/en/ai-war-cloud-database/) Ars Electronica piece by [Sarah Ciston](https://sarahciston.com/) that illustrates the complex relations of AI technology across organizations in industry and government. For exhibition, I created custom XR views for the immersive view, room-sized 3D view of a force directed graph using a bespoke rendering technique.
