Personal websites are modern calling cards, places for expression and flair.

**Like self-realization, it is a constant work in progress.**

This is the seventh major version of this website: designed for:
- Conveniently share things (CV, bio, links, projects) that others may be trying to find. 
- Get my favorite XR and 2D demos in one place so I don't have to type as much when showing them on other devices!
- My design research and VR development involve technical skills in WebXR, Three.js, and scientific visualization. Many of these skills come from training and support by creative coders, artists, and open source developers. I want the landing page's interactivity to bridge both.

This website uses the [Nunito Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter) and is intended to be legible on a variety of 2D and XR devices. 

The [source code](https://github.com/smrghsh/samir-is-on-the-internet) is viewable on GitHub. I use Vue.js + Vite via [Nuxt](https://nuxt.com/) for a content management system and build tooling. The site is hosted with Cloudflare Pages. (Thank you for the configuration help, [Leo](https://github.com/TheInnerParty)!)
