A collaborative WebXR viewer for the massive RYR1 protein, built for the Logitech Dev Studio 2026 competition. Uses the MX Ink stylus to let researchers tap individual amino acid residues in shared VR and inspect binding pocket metadata — residue number, chain, charge, polarity, size. Built on Three.js and Brahma.js with custom rendering techniques for bioinformatics data at scale.
